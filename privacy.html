Privacy Policy.

Last updated: March 11, 2026

The short version: Score. stores all your data privately on your own device and iCloud account. We do not collect, see, or store your financial data on our servers — ever.

1. Introduction

Score. ("the App") is a reseller profit tracker developed by Justin. This Privacy Policy describes how the App handles your information. Score. is built on a privacy-first foundation. Your inventory, sales, and financial records are yours — stored on your device and synced privately through your personal iCloud account.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

Score. does not:

  • Collect or transmit your inventory, sales, or financial data to any server.
  • Sell your personal information to any third party.
  • Use third-party advertising networks or tracking SDKs.
  • Use third-party analytics services (e.g., Firebase, Mixpanel).

3. Your Data & iCloud / CloudKit

Score. uses Apple's SwiftData framework to store all data locally on your device. Sync across your devices is handled via your personal iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit framework. This means your data is stored in your private iCloud container, not a shared or developer-controlled database.

4. Subscriptions & RevenueCat

Score. uses RevenueCat to manage in-app subscriptions and purchases. RevenueCat processes limited purchase-related data (such as purchase tokens) to validate your subscription. RevenueCat does not have access to your inventory, sales, or financial data.

5. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or the way Score. handles your data, please reach out via email at support@scoreapp.pro.
