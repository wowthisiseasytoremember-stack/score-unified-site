Common Inquiries.

How secure is my financial data?

Score. uses on-device encryption. We have zero access to your database. Your data is stored in your private iCloud container, accessible only by you.

Score. is designed as a mobile-first native experience for iPhone and iPad, ensuring you have financial intelligence everywhere you source.

Can I export my data for taxes?

Yes. Pro users can generate standardized CSV and PDF reports compatible with most major accounting software and tax professionals.
