Common Inquiries.

How secure is my data?

Score. uses on-device encryption. Your data is stored in your private iCloud container, accessible only by you. We have no backend servers storing your financial records.

Is there a desktop version?

Score. is currently available for iPhone and iPad, providing a native experience wherever you source inventory.

Can I export my data?

Yes. Pro users can export their entire inventory and sales history to CSV format for accounting and tax purposes.

How do I get help?

Reach out to us at support@scoreapp.pro. We aim to respond to all inquiries within one business day.
